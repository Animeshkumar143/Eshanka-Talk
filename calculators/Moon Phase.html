What is Moon Phase?

The Moon phase refers to the appearance of the Moon as seen from Earth. It changes throughout the month due to the relative positions of the Sun, Earth, and Moon. Understanding your Moon phase in your birth chart can provide insights into your emotional nature and inner world.
